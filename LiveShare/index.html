ABOUT THE CONFERENCE

2025 URS International Multidisciplinary Research and
Extension Advocacy Conference for Champions of Change (IMREACH)
October 10-11, 2025 URS International Multidisciplinary Research

The University of Rizal System deems it essential to sustain its efforts on the internationalization of operations. The twin functions of research and extension are very significant and therefore keeping abreast on the new developments will be of great help to the academic community. The continued dissemination of significant information will therefore ensure that academic, research and training institutions are making scholarly outputs and community development outcomes accessible to stakeholders. The 2025 URS International Multidisciplinary Research and Extension Advocacy Conference for Champions of Change (IMREACH) intends to serve as an avenue for researchers and extension practitioners across disciplines to share relevant information, research outputs and best practices in community development. This will benefit the interested groups with enhanced understanding and appreciation of research development and extension services not only as institutional mandates but as commitment to help improve life. The international multidisciplinary research and extension advocacy conference for champions of change will give opportunity to widen prospects, strengthen partnerships and gain knowledge on innovative and scaled up initiatives for more empowered communities.

In particular, this will:

1. highlight the significance of scaled up efforts in the internationalization of research and extension services;
2. provide a platform on which service learning can be realized through the presentation of qualified papers in research and extension;
3. address global sustainability challenges with research and extension for a more progressive society, and;
4. promote inter/multi-disciplinary approaches in research and extension undertakings.

ABSTRACT FORMAT

1. The Abstract (a 150-300 word informative digest of objectives, methodology, and significant findings of the paper, single-spaced with 3-5 keywords).
2. Abstract shall be submitted through the link: HERE
3. The authors will be notified if their papers are accepted for presentation.
4. File Name Format: Research_Thematic Area_Institution_Name

FORMAT FOR RESEARCH PAPER REPORT

The paper should be typed double-spaced in A4 paper using the Arial font with a size of 11 and with a 25.4 cm margin on all sides. The paper should include the title, author/s, abstract (not more than 300 words), introduction, objectives, review of literature, conceptual framework, theoretical framework, methodology (research design, participants, procedure, & data analysis), results and discussion, implications/ recommendations, acknowledgment, and references. All pages of the paper should be numbered consecutively and should not be more than 30 pages.

PAPER PRESENTATION FORMAT

Only the study leader or authorized representative may present their research. Ten (10) minutes will be allocated for the pre-recorded presentation and another ten (10) minutes for the Q&A portion. Maximum of ten (10) slides only and should contain the following:

  • Title, Proponents, Duration, Budget, Implementing/
    Collaborating Agency
  • Introduction
  • Objectives (in bullet form)
  • Conceptual Framework
  • Methodology (by Objective)
  • Results and Discussions (in bullet form)
  • Implications and Recommendations (in bullet form)

E-POSTER PRESENTATION GUIDELINES

(For Both Research and Extension)

  1. The e-poster must be in video format. Video: in Microsoft formats (i.e. .asf, .wmv, .mp4)
  2. The e-poster will be displayed in the poster loop played online. Multimedia (picture/video) can be integrated into the e-poster showing animations, simulations, or other content. The total playing time must not exceed 3 minutes of duration.
  3. Each e-poster presentation must contain the following: category (as to whether it is a research or extension project), title of the paper, author(s), affiliation(s), rationale, objectives, conceptual/development framework, methodology, results, conclusion, acknowledgment.
  4. Layout:
    • Size: 16x9 aspect ratio
    • Minimum Font 28
    • Font type: Arial, Calibri, Times New Roman
  5. File Name Format: Category_Thematic Area_Institution_Name
